Home

The Summer Mathematics Competitions is an annual online math contest held during the summer for all aspiring math enthusiasts. Consisting of one computational and two proof tests, it is designed to emulate the difficulty and exam format of the American Mathematics Competition series.

The SMC strives to promote mathematical interest in students of all ages, especially those in high school, by providing a fun and fair competition experience. In order to help participants improve their mathematical ability, we release rigorously detailed solutions packets and also provide feedback for the two proof contests.
